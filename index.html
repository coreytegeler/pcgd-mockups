The Purchase College
Graphic Design Dept. We offer a distinctive community in the art and practice of print and digitally based graphic design and experimental publishing. The program teaches students to solve visual communication problems with words, symbols, and image blocks through commercial, political, educational, literary, subversive, personal, and experimental applications. Our faculty members are practicing professional artists with significant exhibition records and prestigious awards. The department's alumni pursue careers in galleries and museums, national publications, art direction, education, and graduate study and research, and as independent, exhibiting studio artists. The graphic design area offers a distinctive program in the art and practice of print- and digitally based graphic design and experimental publishing. The program approaches graphic design as a vital field of professional art practice that gives shape to culture through a variety of media, including posters, motion graphics, books, web and interactive design, type design, exhibition design and packaging.
