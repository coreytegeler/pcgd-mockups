GRAPHIC

The Purchase College graphic design department offers a distinctive program in the art and practice of print and digitally based graphic design and experimental publishing. The program teaches students to solve visual communication problems with words, symbols, and images through commercial, political, educational, literary, subversive, personal, and experimental applications. Our faculty members are practicing professional artists with significant exhibition records and prestigious awards. The department's alumni pursue careers in galleries and museums, national publications, art direction, education, and graduate study and research, and as independent, exhibiting studio artists.

Our faculty members are practicing professional artists with significant exhibition records and prestigious awards. Many are represented by galleries in New York, and many have written and published books. Their work can be found in private, corporate, and museum collections, including the Guggenheim Museum, Metropolitan Museum of Art, Museum of Modern Art, Smithsonian Institution, Victoria and Albert Museum, and Whitney Museum of American Art.
