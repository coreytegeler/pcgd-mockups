Our students approach graphic design as a vital field of professional art practice that gives shape to culture through media. The faculty offer a distinctive program in the art and practice of print and digitally based graphic design and experimental publishing. The program approaches graphic design as a vital field of professional art practice that gives shape to culture through a variety of media: books, magazines, motion graphics, web design, type design, exhibition design, packaging.

Our faculty members are practicing professional artists with significant exhibition records and prestigious awards. Many are represented by galleries in New York, and many have written and published books. Their work can be found in private, corporate, and museum collections, including the Guggenheim Museum, Metropolitan Museum of Art, Museum of Modern Art, Smithsonian Institution, Victoria and Albert Museum, and Whitney Museum of American Art.
